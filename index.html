Amatuer Game Development

Hi! My name is Molly Hutchinson! Sometimes I do some game dev on the sides. I'm still learning, but it's fun to start with a small idea and watch it grow into something that you can play and test around on. Right now I'm working on two games, one is a Gameboy game called Apollyon of Blue Room (not the final name) and the other is a game developed in GameMaker Studio 2 and is called Fodder for the Elder Gods. I'm also in the process of planning another game, which is untitled as of right now.

Apollyon of Blue Room deals with feelings of isolation, anxiety, and depression along with not feeling like yourself anymore.

Fodder for the Elder Gods is a farming game along the lines of Stardew Valley and Harvest Moon but with a focus on aliens and the unknown. You play as an alien that moved to the new planet to spite his family.

ALL images provided are made by me or a couple of my friends that do some of the art for my games and my friends gave me permission to share their work.
